# DELHI ONE — Smart Public Grievance & Community Governance Platform

DELHI ONE is an AI-powered public grievance and civic engagement platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens, local communities, and government authorities. The platform enables people to report local issues such as road damage, water leakage, electricity faults, garbage accumulation, unsafe routes, and other civic problems directly through the app.

The system intelligently auto-updates reported issues on both the application dashboard and Google Maps integration to provide safer routing and real-time awareness for citizens. Community members can vote on reported grievances, helping authorities identify high-priority problems faster and ensuring transparent issue resolution.

The platform uses Artificial Intelligence to detect duplicate complaints, merge similar reports, and reduce spam, making grievance management efficient and organized. DELHI ONE follows a community-driven structure where issues are first handled at the Society level, then escalated to City, State, and broader administrative levels when necessary.

A dedicated government awareness and notification system keeps users informed about ongoing government work, schemes, local developments, infrastructure projects, and public notices in their area. This helps citizens stay connected with real civic progress and utilize available government services effectively.

## Key Features

* AI-based duplicate grievance detection
* Real-time issue reporting & tracking
* Google Maps integration for safe routing
* Community voting & priority-based resolution
* Society → City → State level community structure
* Government work & public updates notification system
* Transparent and citizen-driven governance model
* Smart categorization of civic issues
* Public engagement and awareness platform

## Vision

DELHI ONE aims to create a transparent, technology-driven, and community-powered ecosystem where every citizen can actively participate in improving their locality and monitoring public development work.
